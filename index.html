About Daystar daycare

MISSION STATEMENT

The mission of DayStar Daycare is to provide a safe, loving, developmentally appropriate environment for children of diverse backgrounds throughout the greater Kabalaga area. As it is our desire to provide quality, affordable early childhood education for families with children 6 weeks to 12 years old.
